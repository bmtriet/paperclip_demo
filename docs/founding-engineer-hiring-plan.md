# Founding Engineer Hiring Plan And Recruiting Kickoff

## Hiring Goal
Hire one founding engineer who can own end-to-end product delivery, move quickly with limited process overhead, and partner directly with CTO/CEO on roadmap and architecture.

## Role Scope
- Build and ship product features across frontend, backend, and infrastructure.
- Own technical decisions with strong bias for pragmatic delivery.
- Establish initial engineering quality bar: testing depth, observability, incident response habits, and code review discipline.
- Collaborate with founders on product tradeoffs, customer feedback loops, and prioritization.

## Candidate Profile
- 5-10 years hands-on software engineering experience at startups or high-velocity teams.
- Proven ability to ship full-stack product increments with measurable impact.
- Strong ownership mindset, clear communication, and comfort with ambiguity.
- Experience with at least one modern JS/TS full-stack stack plus cloud deployment.
- Bonus: prior experience as first/early engineering hire.

## Compensation And Package Guidance
- Market-competitive base salary benchmarked to US startup seed/series-A stage.
- Equity meaningful enough to reflect founding-level impact.
- Standard startup benefits plus explicit remote-work flexibility expectations.
- Final package calibrated by level and interview signal consistency.

## Interview Process (Target: 12 calendar days)
1. Recruiter/Founder Screen (30 min)
- Validate motivation, startup fit, communication, and compensation alignment.

2. Technical Screen (60 min)
- Real-world coding and debugging exercise focused on implementation quality and speed.

3. System/Product Collaboration Round (60 min)
- Design and tradeoff session on likely company roadmap features.

4. Founder Values/Ownership Round (45 min)
- Assess autonomy, accountability, conflict handling, and decision quality.

5. Final Debrief And Offer Decision (same day)
- Structured scorecard review and explicit hire/no-hire with risk notes.

## Scorecard Rubric
Use 1-5 scale across these competencies:
- Technical execution depth
- Product judgment
- Architecture and reliability thinking
- Ownership and autonomy
- Communication and collaboration
- Startup resilience and pace

Hire decision rule:
- No score below 3 in core competencies.
- At least two independent strong-hire signals from technical rounds.
- Any critical concern must include mitigation plan or becomes no-hire.

## Sourcing Strategy (First 2 weeks)
- Founders' network outreach: target 25 candidates.
- Curated outbound (LinkedIn/GitHub/communities): target 60 candidates.
- Referrals from trusted operators/investors: target 15 introductions.
- High-signal communities/jobs posts: target 3-5 channels.

## Funnel Targets
- Outreach sent: 100
- Positive replies: 20+
- Screens completed: 10+
- Full loops: 5+
- Offer-ready finalists: 2+
- Offer accepted: 1

## Recruiting Operating Cadence
- Daily: candidate pipeline update and response SLA under 24 hours.
- Twice weekly: hiring standup (CTO + founder stakeholders).
- Weekly: funnel metrics review and messaging calibration.
- Per candidate: debrief completed within 24 hours of last interview.

## Kickoff Checklist (Execution)
- Finalize role narrative, must-haves, and compensation band.
- Publish role brief and outbound message templates.
- Align interview panel and availability blocks for next 3 weeks.
- Prepare coding and system-design prompts with scoring anchors.
- Start outreach wave 1 (minimum 40 prospects in first 72 hours).
- Track funnel in shared sheet/dashboard with stage timestamps.
- Define offer approval path and turnaround SLA (<48 hours).

## Risks And Mitigations
- Risk: low response rate from top-tier candidates.
  - Mitigation: tighten narrative, increase warm intros, shorten reply times.
- Risk: interview bottlenecks from founder calendar constraints.
  - Mitigation: pre-block interview windows and enable delegate interviewers.
- Risk: inconsistent evaluation quality.
  - Mitigation: enforce scorecard rubric and documented debrief template.

## Owners
- CTO: process ownership, technical evaluation bar, final technical recommendation.
- CEO/Founder: narrative, close process, compensation and final offer decision.
- Recruiting support (if available): sourcing ops, scheduling, candidate comms.

## Definition Of Done
- Hiring plan documented and approved.
- Kickoff assets prepared (outbound copy, scorecards, interview prompts).
- Initial sourcing wave launched with trackable funnel metrics.
- First candidate screens scheduled.
